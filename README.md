# 📸 ImageGen – AI Image Generator

Transform your ideas into stunning visuals with the power of AI.  
ImageGen lets you **create, customize, and bring imagination to life** in seconds.

**URL**:https://imagicraft-ai-lab.lovable.app

---

## 🚀 Features

- **AI-Powered Creativity**  
  Generate unique, high-quality images directly from text descriptions using advanced AI models.

- **Lightning Fast**  
  Produce visuals in seconds—no waiting hours for rendering.

- **Unlimited Styles**  
  From realistic photography to abstract art, explore limitless creative possibilities.

- **Beginner-Friendly Interface**  
  Simple, intuitive design that anyone can master in minutes.

---

## 🛠️ Getting Started

1. **Sign Up / Sign In**  
   Access the app via Imagicraft AI Lab [(imagicraft-ai-lab.lovable.app in Bing)](https://www.bing.com/search?q="https%3A%2F%2Fimagicraft-ai-lab.lovable.app%2Fauth").

2. **Enter Your Prompt**  
   Describe the image you want (e.g., *“A futuristic city skyline at sunset”*).

3. **Generate & Customize**  
   Instantly receive AI-generated visuals. Adjust styles, refine prompts, and iterate.

4. **Download & Share**  
   Save your creations or share them with your community.
